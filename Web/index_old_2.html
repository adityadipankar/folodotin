All our work→

We‘re a bunch of artists who love to play with information. Our work spans from packing dense narratives into visual identities to visualising the narrative itself.

We work with startups in helping their ideas grow exponentially, by providing them impetus’ in reaching their customers and creating engaging experiences for their products.

FOLO’s alter ego is musical. We work with electronic and classical media to compose ambient and fusion music.
