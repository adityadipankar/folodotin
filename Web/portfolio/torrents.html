The peer is a circular body, divided into halves by it's property of being a seeder and a leecher, at the same time.

Every peer is a node in this universe of the torrent network, contributing in the upload of a torrent somewhere and also leeching from various sources (seeders).

Public torrent aggregator Torrentz is currently indexing 11.70 million torrents (without duplicates) across 33 domains.
