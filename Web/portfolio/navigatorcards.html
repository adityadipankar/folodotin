Navigator cards for QuestAlliance

QuestAlliance helps the urban newbies cope with the city life and in this process, they have created programs through which trainers speak to the students and disburse carefully drafted lessons. The trainer is called as a ‘Navigator’.

The cards talk about the various aspects of a navigator's role in the development of a student.

The most important aspect of the journal was the attendance sheet, where the parents are supposed to encirlce their child's attendance in school, and in case of absence, also explain the reason of absence.

Along with the cards, there was a simple sheet, also provided, to asses the learning of a student over the duration of the course.

Many people from the villages are migrating to cities and the environment is very different.
