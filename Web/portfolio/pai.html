Bicycles are bought in boxes and carried to places, and very often, there is a need of a mechanic to help with the assembly. This quick starter guide helps anyone understand how to put together their bike, very easily.

Labelled steps, with the screws and motion of parts.

A user should be able to quickly put together the bicycle himself, after he opens the box.
