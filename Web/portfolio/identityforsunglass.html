Beehive - An identity concept for Sunglass

← all projects

Sunglass is a beehive. A place where smart and hard working professionals collect their experiences and combine them with that of others. A high energy structure that churns out things that amaze and benefit masses.

Inspired by a beehive, the geometry of the logo and the simplicity of the hexagonal structure.

Sunglass will make it easy for anyone to work with teams on 3D projects.
