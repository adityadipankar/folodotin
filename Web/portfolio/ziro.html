Ziro - A Cafe in Hauz Khas Village

← all projects

 ↓ ↑ 

Using interior elements from the cafe to create signage-identity. The cafe space is situated in a little 'village' within the city, which is cutoff from its mundane hustle by a reservoir, a park and a single stretch leading to the heart of this village. The place holds prominence for its cafes, boutiques, galleries and restaurants.

The idea was to portray a scene from the cafe while displaying all signage points as objects in the scene. Details such as worn walls, windows, hacked furniture, a guitar, kite light were used to display objects inside the cafe which maybe interesting for new customers to visually register, and regular customers to appreciate as passive identity. Line drawing and washed out colors were used to communicate jaded and homely,warm-yellow light feeling.

Ziro is perhaps, the most inviting and open, of all cafes in Hauz Khas Village.
